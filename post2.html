Why Web Design is Important

by Delaney Harrison

Posted on March 12, 2020

Why Web Design?

66% of people would rather see something well designed online versus something plain and simple if given a short period of time to view the website. Your website’s design is an very important part of your online presence, and can be essential to building a business. In order to build a successful website for your business, you must first understand the importance of web design and how it applies to our world.

The following list are a few key essential points as to why web design is so important, and why it is so desperately needed.

  • SEO (Search Engine Optimization), when searching for your business/organization your website will come up!
  • Shows how you accomodate to your customers and how well you may treat them according to a study
  • It sets the first impression, how do you want your business to be viewed?
  • Put up a fight to your competitors, you want to win over your competition! Give your users and consumers just reason to use you over someone else
  • Build trust with your users/customers
  • Stay consistent, provide a great overall experience!
  • Increased web traffic
  • Better usability
  • It just looks better... enough said

Important qualities of good web design:

  • Universal toolkit for use across pages
  • Easy navigation
  • Responsive Design
  • Page speed
  • Interactivity
  • CTA (Call to Action) Buttons
