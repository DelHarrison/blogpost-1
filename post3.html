The Oculus Quest

by Delaney Harrison

Posted on March 21, 2020

Oculus Quest

I personally love virtual reality. Any chance I can get to get my hands on a headset, I will take. I recently was able to get the Oculus Quest and be able to play some games such as Beat Saber and Vader Immortal Episode I.

I have loved every experience I have had with this technology, however I will be listing some pros and cons for this product.

Pros:

  • No wires! Easier to move around
  • Camera for being able to see with the headset on while making the boundary
  • Boundary memory storage for the space you are in
  • Easy to download games, using the Oculus app on your phone
  • The audience can view what the player is seeing through casting onto the phone

Cons:

  • Battery life, only lasts about 1.5 hours
  • Limited Storage
  • Stay away from sunlight! The quest may misinterpret the boundaries for the guaridan
  • Expensive: ~$300
  • Limited games, yet to be developed for Quest
